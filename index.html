Zero-ops OpenStack on just about any Linux box.

A full OpenStack in a single snap package. MicroStack is an upstream single-node OpenStack deployment which can run directly on your workstation. Although made for developers, it is also suitable for edge, IoT and appliances. Grab MicroStack from the Snap Store and get your OpenStack running right away.

Reliable, small, ready to go

  • Secure

    Pure upstream OpenStack delivered to your laptop.
